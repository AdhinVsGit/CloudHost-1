Kick Buttowski

About Kick

Clarence "Kick" Buttowski, the suburban daredevil who aspires to become the world's greatest stuntman, set himself a new challenge crazier than ever! He wants this time to hit the terrible slopes of Dead Man's Drop and Mt. Hurtsmore, with only his white helmet with red stripes to protect him. On foot, bike, skateboard or kart, help our Disney XD hero to survive this hell while performing epic stunts.
